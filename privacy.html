Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What data we collect

When you use the contact form on our website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The message you send us

How we use your data

We use this information only to respond to your inquiry. We do not use it for marketing, profiling, or any automated decision-making.

Where your data is stored

Your message is sent to Formspree, a third-party form processing service, and forwarded to our email. Formspree processes data in accordance with their own privacy policy. We retain inquiry emails for as long as needed to address your request, and delete them afterward.

Cookies

This website does not set any cookies. We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or any third-party scripts that collect personal data beyond the contact form described above.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Contact

For any questions or requests regarding your data, please reach out via our contact form or email us directly.
